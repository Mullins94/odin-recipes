Chickpea Shakshuka√ü

Description:

A chickpea dish that works for breakfast, lunch or dinner. Chickpeas are the main source of protein. Can be served with any carb you choose, crusty bread or pita recommended.

Ingredients

  • 1 Can Chickpeas
  • 1 Jar Marinara sauce
  • Bread or other Carb
  • 1 Clove Garlic
  • Spinach or Kale Bunch

Steps

  1. Add the garlic and chickpeas to a pan on medium heat.
  2. Put bread in the oven to toast.
  3. Wilt the spinach> or kale> in the pan.
  4. Add the marinara and simmer on medium high heat.
  5. Once thick, remove bread from oven and turn off the heat. Serve Shakshuka with the bread.
