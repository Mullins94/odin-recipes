Red Lentil Dhal

Description:

A curry dish with lentils as the main protein. Can be served over rice or with pita.

Ingredients

  • 300g Red Lentils
  • 1 Brick of Frozen Spinach
  • Veggie Stock
  • 1 Clove Garlic
  • Curry Powder
  • Water

Steps

  1. Add the all ingredients to a pot.
  2. Simmer on medium high heat for 15 minutes.
  3. Prepare rice or pita for the side.
  4. Once thick remove Dhal from heat to cool.
  5. Serve with side.
