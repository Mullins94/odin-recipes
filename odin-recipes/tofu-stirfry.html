Tofu Stirfry

Description:

Easy tofu stirfry served over rice.

Ingredients

  • 31 Brick Tofu
  • Broccoli
  • Rice
  • Soya Sauce

Steps

  1. Fry the tofu in a pan.
  2. Cook the rice once tofu is cooking.
  3. Add broccoli to pan with tofu.
  4. Add soya sauce to pan and allow to thicken.
  5. Stir rice into the pan.
  6. Serve when sauce is thick and everything is cooked.
